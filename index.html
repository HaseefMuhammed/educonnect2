Why Is It Special

User-Friendly Interface

We design intuitive and accessible tools that enable teachers to interact with EDU BOT AI effortlessly, ensuring a seamless experience when moving the AI teacher, joining EB STREAM, adding files to EB FILES, and chatting with students through EB CHAT.

Robust Functionality

Our powerful platform supports a wide range of functions, allowing teachers to perform tasks like accessing all passwords, viewing EDU BOT AI activities, and facilitating smooth communication and file sharing, all within a single app.

Highly Customizable

EDU CONNECT offers extensive customization options, allowing teachers to tailor the app to their specific needs. This flexibility ensures that each feature, from managing student interactions to monitoring EDU BOT AI, can be personalized for an optimal teaching experience.

Specially build for EDU BOT AI

EDU CONNECT is designed exclusively for EDU BOT AI, providing teachers with seamless control, resource management, and communication tools to enhance the educational experience.
